About Us

Techy Jaunt is a unique marketplace, that provides a transparent, fast and secure environment for the seamless exchange and trading of Non Fungible Tokens (NFTs) and assets eg Art, Music, Properties, etc and digital assets, leveraging on the possibilities of the Blockchain technology and Web3.0.

Diverse Asset Options for Investing and Trading

We are not limiting our service to only creators of digital artworks and paintings but we offer diverse asset options. Our marketplace is open to owners of physical properties, real estate, sports merchandise, fashion clothing, Song records videos, physical items, etc

Barter Exchange of Assets. Exchange with Peers

Techy Jaunt's Marketplace offers a barter trading feature that enables a user to exchange one class of NFT or assets for another.

Crypto and Fiat Payment Options

Purchase an NFT and any digital asset with cryptocurrencies and acceptable fiat currencies e.g USD($)

Incentivised Royalty and Earning Structure for Creators

List and Sell your Property on Techy Jaunt NFT.

Fractional Ownership of Assets

At Techy Jaunt you can own properties, digital artworks, music, etc in simple fractions of part which enhances fundraising and revenue generation.
Own Equity in properties and assets just by owning a small fraction of a property or asset.

Low Transaction

At Techy Jaunt we offer a 2% transactional cost on every purchase and sale made on our marketplace

Purpose

At Techy Jaunt we provide solutions to improve the NFT marketplace model. We are resolving the exclusion of diverse assets of different class from benefiting from Blockchain technology and Tokenization. We are also resolving the exclusion of low income earners who have the ambition of being owners of property, homes, real estate, Art, collectibles etc, but cannot afford to purchase properties at asking prices. We are solving the issue of weak compensation and royalty framework for creators who own NFTs and desire better compensation and protection of their works. We are also facilitating the quicker exchange of NFTs in a faster and more secure manner, with low transactional fees.

Solutions

  • List and Sell your physical property, Art, Music, etc, and digital assets as NFTs.
  • Alternative payment options: Fiat/Crypto.
  • Barter your NFTs and assets.
  • Fractional Ownership of Assets made possible
